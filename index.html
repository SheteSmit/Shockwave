Shockwave

This is the official website for Shockwave, the discord bot for League Team Management.

At the moment, this site is under construction. But you can add our bot at https://tinyurl.com/AddShockwave

The bot is currently in its alpha testing phase, so if you find any issues or want to suggest a feature, please post about it on our GitHub: https://github.com/SuyogKhanal5/LeagueTeamManager

You can post in the discussions tab, or open up an issue if you want to let us know of any bugs or features.
